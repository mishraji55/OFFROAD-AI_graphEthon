## Description of the App

OFFROAD-AI is an AI-powered off-road navigation and terrain understanding system designed to assist vehicles in navigating challenging, unstructured environments. Unlike traditional navigation systems that rely on mapped roads, OFFROAD-AI analyzes real-time environmental data and terrain characteristics to determine safe and optimal paths.

The system leverages computer vision, AI/ML models, and environmental sensing to classify terrain types, detect obstacles, and adapt to changing environmental conditions. This makes it suitable for autonomous vehicles, rescue missions, agricultural vehicles, military applications, and exploration in areas where road infrastructure is unavailable.

The application processes real-time inputs and provides terrain-level understanding, environmental awareness, and intelligent decision support for safer off-road navigation.

---

## Features

### 1. Terrain Level Understanding

- Real-time terrain classification  
- Identifies surfaces such as mud, sand, gravel, rocks, and water  
- Pixel-level segmentation for accurate terrain mapping  
- Predicts navigability and risk levels  
- Helps vehicles select safest route dynamically  

### 2. Environmental Changes

- Detects weather and lighting variations  
- Adapts to fog, rain, shadows, and night conditions  
- Identifies dynamic obstacles  
- Updates terrain understanding in real time  
- Improves decision-making in unpredictable environments
